WhiteSand Research, LLC

WhiteSand Research, LLC is a minority owned full-service company that offers research and consulting solutions, as well as recruiting services, to the financial services industry, including institutional investors, hedge funds, and investment firms.

The company was founded in 2006 as a boutique research group, focusing on producing proprietary research reports on publicly-traded companies facing event-driven catalysts, including mergers and acquisitions, corporate buyback situations, turnarounds, spin-offs, post-bankruptcies and capital restructurings.

Producing quality, in-depth and unbiased research to help our clients shape their investment processes has been the cornerstone of our companyâ€™s success. WhiteSand employs a value-oriented, event-driven investment strategy that seeks to capitalize on the most inefficient and attractive investment opportunities.

Building on our strong and trusted relationships with our clients, WhiteSand now provides talent acquisition services to the financial services industry. With over 10 years of experience working with financial services executives and money managers, we are able to quickly understand a firm's strategy, culture, and their ideal candidate.

WhiteSand manages the full life cycle of the talent acquisition process from open requisition to offer stage, including: job specification and development, screening and qualifying top candidates, interviewing and negotiating offers.
